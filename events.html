Honoring Excellence in Global Marketing

The Kotler Awards have been created in Prof. Philip Kotler's honour, The Father of Modern Marketing, to recognize and honour the world's best marketing and business management experts. It honours those who have displayed notable and sensational practices in the sector as well as notable participants from various agencies.

29th FEB, 2020

Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi

In the rapidly changing market environment, this award also aimed to highlight the direction of the most logical but appropriate marketing initiatives. Psyche Panacea had the opportunity to serve as an Outreach partner for The Kotler Awards in India. It was both an honour and a privilege for Psyche Panacea to collaborate for Kotler Awards as outreach partners.
