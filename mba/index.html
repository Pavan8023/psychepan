Global MBA Program Details

8-Months Fast-Track MBA Program

Our 8-month fully online MBA program is delivered in collaboration with accredited UK universities. It consists of 4 months of interactive online modules (8 in total, each ending with a Reflective Thinking Report) followed by automatic enrollment at one of our UK university partners for the Master's Thesis.

Special Intakes

Special intakes can be arranged for 12+ participants, and we offer customization with modules such as Purpose-Driven Leadership (Simon Sinek) and Female Leadership in Entrepreneurship.

Co-Branding Options

While the MBA degree itself is not co-brandable, we can co-brand the Level 7 Diploma in Strategic Management & Leadership if required.

Delivery Locations

Programs can be delivered in-house at the client's location, or at our university sites in Turkey, Hamburg, Switzerland, or London, depending on preferences.

Executive Leadership Program

This consists of 2–3 days of in-person training with me in the client's country (e.g., Malaysia, Kuwait), followed by a module at one of our elite partner universities – such as Oxford, LSE, LBS, Imperial College London or, most affordably, Copenhagen Business School.
