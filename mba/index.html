Admissions & Program Details

8-Month Fast-Track MBA

Our 8-month fully online MBA program is delivered in collaboration with accredited UK universities. It consists of 4 months of interactive online modules (8 in total, each ending with a Reflective Thinking Report) followed by seamless enrollment at one of our UK university partners for the Master's Thesis.

Private Cohorts

Private cohorts available for 12+ participants, and we offer customization with modules such as Purpose-Driven Leadership (Simon Sinek) and Female Leadership in Entrepreneurship.

Co-Branding Options

While the MBA degree itself is not co-brandable, co-brand option available for the Level-7 Diploma in Strategic Management & Leadership if required.

Delivery Locations

Programs can be delivered in-house at the client's location, or at our university sites in Turkey, Hamburg, Switzerland, or London, depending on preferences.

Executive Leadership Program

This consists of 2–3 days of in-person training with me in the client's country (e.g., Malaysia, Kuwait), followed by a module at one of our elite partner universities – such as Oxford, LSE, LBS, Imperial College London or, most affordably, Copenhagen Business School.

Strategic Clarity

Develop clear strategic direction and decision-making capabilities.

Negotiation Mastery

Master advanced negotiation techniques for business success.

Purpose-Driven Decisions

Align business decisions with core purpose and values.

Implementation Roadmap

Create actionable implementation plans for sustainable growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What is the duration of the MBA program?

The Global MBA is an 8-month fast-track program consisting of 4 months of interactive online modules followed by seamless enrollment at a UK university for the Master's Thesis.

Is the program entirely online?

Yes, the core program is 100% online. However, we offer optional European study modules for those who prefer a blended learning experience.

What are the entry requirements?

We consider both academic qualifications and professional experience. In specific cases, entry is possible without a traditional bachelor's degree based on substantial professional achievement.

When can I start the program?

We have monthly rolling intakes. Private cohorts can be arranged for groups of 12+ participants.

What you will get?

Dual Certification: Earn two certificates—one from a world- renowned academic institution (e.g., Imperial College London or Oxford) and one from the Opresnik Business School, showcasing your leadership excellence.
